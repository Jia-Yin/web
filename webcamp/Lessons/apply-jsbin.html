# Apply for a jsbin account

  1. Go to https://jsbin.com.
  2. Click Login or Register at right top corner.

  1. If you have a Github account, then login. Otherwise, click Create an account.

  1. Fill the form for registration. Then click Verify account.

  1. Rotate the picture to upward.

  1. Then click Create ac account again.

  1. Choose Free account plan.

  1. You need to verify the account by email.

  1. Login to your Gibhub account and Authorize jsbin.

  1. Check your jsbin account.
