# Thank You

This tutorial series are first designed for a Web Programming Camp in 2019/07. To prepare this camp, I invited five students to join the lecturer team. We had online meeting once per week for three times, and another two face-to-face meetings to discuss the materials. I think it's better for the students to learn the material first, so I ask them to prepare and report some content at every meeting. At last, I merged all and added my own ideas to complete these tutorials. I want to appreciate my students' help, they are 梁家萁、楊依芳、莊晋翔、余舜華、詹子潔.

This is my first attempt to create such a tutorial series. Thank you for your reading. If you find any mistakes or have any suggestions for modification, please feel free to contact me. My email is jywglady@cycu.org.tw. Thank you.
